Share your building plans

Upload and share your building plans for smart solutions of clustering concepts. Whether it is a cluster of e.g. Intel NUC barebone computers, single-board computers like Raspberry Pis or other beowulf concepts. The building plans should challenge the status-quo while following some basic conceptual quality factors, such as

  • reproducibility
  • understandability
  • maintainability
  • extendibility

Uploaded concepts will be shared on a dedicated sub-section of this page (which is currently in the making).

Share your provisioning source code

Upload, share and demonstrate your provisioning tools and scripts to the world. Be it a smart layout of an Ansible project to provision your cluster with Kubernetes, OpenShift, Docker Swarm or a distributed Filesystem, such as GlusterFS, or a completely different approach. Shared projects should be well documented and the source code should follow some basic quality factors, such as

  • portability & reproducibility
  • understandability
  • maintainability
  • extendibility

Uploaded concepts will be shared on a dedicated sub-section of this page (which is currently in the making).
